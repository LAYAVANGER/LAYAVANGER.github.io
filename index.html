How to do it:

⚠️ Use with caution. Rockstar may patch this glitch or take action against exploiters.

🛫 Cayo Perico Replay Glitch (GTA Online)

How to do it:
1. Complete the Cayo Perico Heist.
2. Before the final cutscene, disconnect your internet or close the game.
3. Restart the game, and the heist should be available again.

⚠️ Repeat sparingly to avoid detection. Always deposit your earnings before repeating.

How to do it:
1. Save Lester's assassination missions until after completing the main story.

🚗 Vehicle Duplication Glitch (GTA Online)

How to do it:
1. Enter your vehicle and drive to a garage.
2. Use the "Export Vehicle" option in the garage.
3. While exporting, exit the game or disconnect internet to trick the game.
4. Upon reloading, your vehicle will be duplicated.

⚠️ Rockstar frequently patches this glitch. Use carefully to avoid bans.
